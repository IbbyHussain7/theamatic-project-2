About Us:

Welcome to the Cluedo Game Companion! We are a group of enthusiastic board game players who wanted to bring the classic murder mystery game Cluedo into the digital age. Our goal is to enhance your gaming experience by providing you with an easy-to-use web tool for tracking, organizing, and enjoying your game sessions. Whether you're a first-time Cluedo player or a seasoned Cluedo detective, our website offers a variety of tools to help you streamline your game:

  • Character Randomizer
  • Weapon and Room Randomizers
  • Move Tracker and Suggestion Log
  • Player Notes Section
We’re continuously improving the website, adding new features, and ensuring that you have the best experience possible. So, get ready to roll the dice, make your suggestions, and solve the murder mystery like never before! Thank you for choosing Cluedo Game Companion—where the clues come to life!
